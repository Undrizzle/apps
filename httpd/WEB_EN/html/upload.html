Update Software

You can complete the upgrade of the software in accordance with the following steps.


Step 1: Obtain an updated software image file from your ISP.

Step 2: Click the "Browse" button to select the image file.

Step 3: Click the "Update Software" button once to upload the new image file.

Note: The update process takes about 2 minutes to complete, and your CBAT will reboot.
